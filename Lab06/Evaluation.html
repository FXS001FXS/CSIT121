Evaluation of Cisco.com

Website Details

  • URL: https://www.cisco.com
  • Name: Cisco
  • Target Audience: IT professionals and business decision-makers.

Site Organization

The site is seemingly well-structured with sections for Products, Solutions, Support, and Resources. The site's accesbility score ranked at 73, having issues mainly around the lack of intergrated accessibility for blind and or deaf people. Many of the pages requre tedious scroll and clicking to reach.

CRAP Design Principles

Utilizes contrast effectively, consistent repetition in layout, clear alignment, and logical proximity of information.

Accessibility & Recommendations

The Cisco Concierge is a rather effective means of navigating the site, yet seeing as its accesbility leaves much to be desired navigation structure for better user experience is paramount.
