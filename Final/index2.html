Evaluation of Cisco.com

Website Details

Lab 4 Lab 6 Lab 5 Lab 10
  • URL: https://www.cisco.com
  • Name: Cisco
  • Target Audience: IT professionals and business decision-makers.

Site Organization

The site is seemingly well-structured with sections for Products, Solutions, Support, and Resources. The site's accessibility score ranked at 73, having issues mainly around the lack of integrated accessibility for blind and/or deaf people. Many of the pages require tedious scrolling and clicking to reach.

CRAP Design Principles

Utilizes contrast effectively, consistent repetition in layout, clear alignment, and logical proximity of information.

Accessibility & Recommendations

The Cisco Concierge is a rather effective means of navigating the site, yet seeing as its accessibility leaves much to be desired, navigation structure for better user experience is paramount.
